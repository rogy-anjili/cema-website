About CEMA

We are a creative education community empowering children and youth to imagine, create, and lead.

🌍 Vision

A creative, literate and empowered generation leading Africa’s future.

🎯 Mission

To inspire and equip children and youth through arts, literacy, storytelling and mentorship.

⭐ Values

Creativity, Inclusion, Integrity, Empowerment, Community.

Our Story

CEMA was founded to create safe spaces where children can explore their talents and develop literacy skills through art and mentorship.

Why CEMA Exists

Many children struggle with literacy not because they lack intelligence, but because they lack supportive spaces, inspiring tools, and creative mentorship. CEMA fills that gap by combining art and education into an engaging experience.
